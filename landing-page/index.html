🎲 Roll Dice – Game Details

Ye 2 player turn-based dice game hai. Goal simple hai Target score pehle complete karo aur jeeto.

🔄 Turn Ka System

  • Har turn me tum dice roll karte ho.
  • Agar 2–6 aaye to wo number tumhare current score me add hota hai.
  • Phir tum decide karte ho:

  • Roll Again

    → aur points lene ki koshish (risk bhi hai 😈)

  • Hold

    → jitne points bane wo total score me save ho jayenge aur turn khatam

❌ Agar 1 aa jaye

  • Tumhara current score zero ho jata hai
  • Turn turant next player ko chala jata hai

🏆 Win Kaise Hoga?

  • Jo player Winning Score pehle complete kare wo jeet jata hai

🔁 New Game

  • New Game karne par sab score zero ho jate hain aur Player 1 se game start hoti hai.
